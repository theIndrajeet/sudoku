Privacy Policy

For Sudoku Free

Effective Date: July 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sudoku Free ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in relation to your use of our mobile application, Sudoku Free (the "App").

Your privacy is important to us. It is our policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our App.

1. Information We Collect

We have designed our App to be a simple and enjoyable experience. The information we collect depends on the features you use.

Note to Developer

Choose the section below that applies to your app. If you do not use any ads or analytics, use Section A and delete Section B. If you use ads or analytics, use Section B and delete Section A.

SECTION A - For Apps WITHOUT Ads or Analytics

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you through the App. All game data, such as your current puzzle, statistics (e.g., best times), and settings, is stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

SECTION B - For Apps WITH Ads or Analytics

To support and improve our App, we work with third-party services for advertising and analytics. These services may collect certain information as described below:

  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about your interaction with the App. This includes which features you use, your game progress, session duration, and crash reports. This data is aggregated and anonymized and helps us understand how users engage with our App so we can fix bugs and improve the user experience.
    Service Example: Google Analytics for Firebase.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal information about the device you are using, such as the device model, operating system version, and screen resolution. This helps us optimize the App for different devices and troubleshoot compatibility issues.
  • Advertising ID: Our third-party advertising partners may collect your device's unique advertising identifier (e.g., GAID on Android). This identifier is used to serve personalized advertisements that are more relevant to your interests. You can typically reset this identifier or opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.
    Service Example: Google AdMob.

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: To save your game progress and settings on your device.
  • To Improve the App: To understand and analyze how you use our App, identify crashes, and make improvements to its functionality and design.
  • To Provide Advertising: To display advertisements within the App, which helps us keep the App free. Our partners may use the information collected to show you personalized ads.

3. Third-Party Services

Our App may use third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services have their own privacy policies that describe how they use your information. We recommend you review their policies.

  • Google Play Services

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our App from loss, theft, and unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
